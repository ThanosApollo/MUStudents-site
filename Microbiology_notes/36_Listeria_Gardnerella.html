36. Listeria, Gardnerella

Listeria Monocytogenes

Found in :

  • Unpasteurized dairy products
  • Cold deli meats
  • Transplacental transmission/vaginal transmission during childbirth

Bacterial cutlure:

  • Facultative anaerobe
  • Facultative intracellular
  • Flaggelated with tumbling motility

Virulence factors:

  • Actin rocket tail
  • Cold resistance

Treatment:

  • Ampicillin

Gardnerella Vaginalis

Found in:

  • normal vaginal flora

Bacterial cutlure:

  • characteristic clue cells
  • Plemorphic
  • gram variable rod
  • KOH whiff test used for diagnostics
  • grows at pH above 4.5

Virulence factors

  • Pili
  • Capsule
  • Phospholipase C
  • Vaginolysin, cytolysin
  • Biofilm formation

Diseases

  • Bacterian vaginosis(pH above 4.5)

Treatment

  • Metronidazole
